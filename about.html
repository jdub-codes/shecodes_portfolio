About me.

Storytelling runs in my blood. I was six years old when my grandfather asked me, "If I wrote a book for you, what would it be about?"

I replied, "Write me a book about a fairy princess." After my grandfather passed, and I was going through his things, and I found the book he wrote for me all those years ago.

And the rest is history. We all have a story to tell and this portfolio is the story of my journey through the SheCodes course.

Learning to code is like writing a novel. There are highs and lows, and you pour your heart and soul into it. There are somes pages missing and others already written.

Like with all good fairly tales, I'm beginning to see my happily ever after. Who knows what my next story will be? I have a feeling it will involve coding.
